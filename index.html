< About Me />

I'm a passionate frontend developer who loves crafting interactive and visually engaging web experiences. My journey started with a curiosity about how websites are built, which naturally led me to learn HTML and CSS — and I’ve been hooked ever since.

Over time, I’ve expanded my skill set to include JavaScript, React, and Python, which allows me to build dynamic, responsive interfaces that are both user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing. I focus on creating smooth, fast, and functional experiences for users.

I've also built three full Shopify e-commerce stores, where I worked on theme customization, product setup, and performance optimization. This hands-on experience taught me how to design not just beautiful websites, but ones that convert and scale effectively.

Every project I work on is guided by clean code practices, mobile-first design, and attention to detail. My goal is always to deliver websites that look great, work seamlessly across devices, and meet the unique needs of each client or brand.
